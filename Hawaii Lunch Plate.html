Hawaii Lunch Plate

This dish can be sonsidered as a multicultural, island-influenced version of traditional Southern meat-and-three plates, and is a cheap plate you can get from almost anywhere on the islands! This carb-loaded plate usually includes mayonnaise-based macaroni salad and two scoops of white rice. The plate lunch is truly one of the best deals and most authentic eats you can get on the islands. It is usually made with two scoops of white rice, macaroni salad and your choice of meat: teriyaki beef, fried chicken, or kalua pork.



Ingredients

  • two scoops of white rice
  • ahi poke
  • lomi-lomi salmon
  • haupia dessert
  • kālua puaʻa (roast pork)
  • pork lau lau


Recipe

  1. 1. For each plate, add rice, macaroni salad and an entree.

  2. 2. You can choose one entree, mix and match 2 of the proteins, or have a little of all three to make a “mixed plate”!
