Thai Tea

Thai Tea is a cold beverage commonly associated with desserts for its refreshing taste. It is a highly popular drink in Southeast China. It is usually made from Ceylon tea, milk and sugar, and served either hot or cold. You may also find orange blossom water, star anise, crushed tamarind seed, and sometimes other spices as well within this drink.You can find this drink in many Thai food resteraunts!



Ingredients

  • 4 cups (960 ml) water
  • 4 organic black tea bags
  • 3/4 cup (150 g) granulated sugar
  • 2 anise stars
  • 1 smashed green cardamom pod
  • 2 whole cloves
  • 1 cup (240 ml) half and half


Recipe

  1. 1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan or tea kettle.

  2. 2. Remove saucepan or kettle from heat. Combine hot water with tea mix and sugar and stir to combine.

  3. 3. Cover and allow tea to steep for 15 minutes.

  4. 4. Strain tea and allow it to cool completely.

  5. 5. Pour tea over ice. Stir in sweetened condensed milk, adding more or less depending
