Daily Dish: Sake

Rating:

Sake is a rice wine brewed by fermenting rice, which turns the starches within the grains into sugar and then alcohol. Sake originated in Japan and can come in a variety of alcohol concentrations, depending on how much it is diluted.


Sake allegedly has health benefits. Sake contains lactobacillus, a lactic acid-producing bacteria that is rumored to help with digestive problems. We highly recommend it!
