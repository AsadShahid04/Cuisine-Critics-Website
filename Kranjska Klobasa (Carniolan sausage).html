Classics

Stefani Pecenka

A food that is basically meat loaf with hard boiled eggs that is simple to make. A beautiful egg ring form in the center of the meat which creates a stunning pattern, that won’t leave you unimpressed. This meatloaf is another classic European food that is worth taking the time to savor the flavor, especially during the colder times of the year.



Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef ground
  • 1 lb pork ground
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 clove garlic minced
  • 5 slices day old bread
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon parsley
  • 2 teaspoons vegeta
  • 5 tablespoons oil 2 for roasting onions, 3 for dripping roast
  • 6 eggs
  • 1/4 cup beef stock


Recipe

  1. 1. Soak the bread slices in milk.

  2. 2. Chop the onions finely and saute in a little oil

  3. 3. Mix the meat and add the soaked bread, all the spices, one egg and the cooked onion and garlic.

  4. 4. Boil and peel the eggs Studd the mold with the eggs by making a tunnel with your hands.

  5. 5. Bake for aroud 1 hour and cut into thick slices. Enjoy!
